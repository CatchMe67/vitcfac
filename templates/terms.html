Terms of Use

Last updated: May 2026

1. What This Platform Is

We are an independent, student-run platform that allows students to anonymously submit structured ratings and pre-defined tags about their academic experience with professors. It is not affiliated with, operated by, or approved by VIT or any educational institution.

2. Who Can Use This Platform

By using this platform, you confirm that you are a currently enrolled or previously enrolled student at the institution this platform covers. You agree not to submit reviews about professors you have not personally had academic interaction with.

3. What You Can and Cannot Submit

You may submit:

  • Star ratings (1–5) across the defined metric categories
  • Pre-defined tags from the provided list
  • A binary verdict (Hell yea/Hell nah) based on your personal academic experience

You may not attempt to:

  • Manipulate ratings through automated scripts, bots, or coordinated mass submissions
  • Submit reviews on behalf of other students
  • Use the platform to target, harass, or defame any individual

4. Nature of Content

All submitted content on this platform is structured and pre-defined — there are no free-text fields. All ratings reflect the personal subjective opinions and academic experiences of individual students. They do not constitute official performance reviews, HR evaluations, or factual statements of fact about any faculty member.

5. No Warranty

This platform is provided as-is, for informational and personal academic decision-making purposes only. We make no guarantees about the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of any rating or aggregate score displayed.

6. Limitation of Liability

The operator of this platform is a private individual and accepts no liability for any decisions made based on content displayed here — including but not limited to course selection, academic planning, or any professional consequences. Use this platform at your own discretion.

7. Takedown Requests

If you are a faculty member and believe information displayed about you is factually incorrect or was submitted in bad faith, you may contact us via the email listed in the footer to request a review. We will investigate and act in good faith.
