About

Last updated: May 2026

What is this platform?

We are a student-built, student-run platform designed to help students make more informed decisions during course registration. It gives you structured, data-driven insights on professors — focused specifically on what matters for your internals and daily academic life, not general impressions.

Why does this exist?

Course registration is essentially blind. You pick a professor without knowing anything about their teaching style, grading approach, or how they treat students. This platform fixes that.

Who built this?

This platform was built independently by a student. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to VIT in any way. The administration, faculty body, and institution had no involvement in its creation.

Is this anonymous?

Yes. There are no accounts, no email sign-ups, and no way to trace a rating back to the student who submitted it. We collect a one-way browser fingerprint only to prevent the same person from rating the same professor multiple times.

Why no free-text reviews?

Because the moment you allow students to type freely, the platform becomes a legal liability and gets shut down. By using only structured ratings and pre-defined tags, every piece of content on this platform is a statistical data point — not a statement — which gives the platform long-term survivability.

How are ratings calculated?

Each metric (Lecture Value, DA Leniency, Assignment Hassle, Vibe Check) is averaged across all submissions for that professor. The percentage score is the ratio of students who voted "Hell yea" on that professor. No weighting or manipulation is applied.
