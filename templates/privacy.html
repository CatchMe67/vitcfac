Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. What We Collect

When you submit a rating, we collect:

  • Your responses to the structured rating form (numeric scores, selected tags, verdict)
  • A browser fingerprint — a non-reversible hash generated from publicly available browser signals (screen size, language, timezone, etc.). This is used only to prevent the same person from submitting multiple reviews for the same professor. It is not linked to your identity.
  • Your IP address, used only for rate-limiting (preventing spam submissions). It is not stored permanently or linked to your review content.
  • Your browser's User-Agent string, for spam detection purposes only.

We do not collect your name, email address, student ID, phone number, or any information that could identify you personally.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require account creation
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not run advertising networks or sell data to third parties
  • We do not use analytics platforms that identify individual users (e.g. Google Analytics with user tracking)

3. How We Use What We Collect

Data Purpose Retention
Rating scores & tags Display aggregated professor stats Indefinitely
Browser fingerprint Prevent duplicate reviews Indefinitely
IP address Rate-limit spam submissions 24 hours max
User-Agent Spam detection 24 hours max

4. Who Can See Your Data

No individual review is traceable back to you. Displayed data is always aggregated (e.g. "4.2 / 5 average across 84 reviews"). The platform operator can see raw database entries, but has no way to identify who submitted which review — the fingerprint is a one-way hash.

5. Third Parties

This platform does not share any collected data with third parties, advertisers, or the institution this platform covers.

6. Your Rights

Since we collect no personally identifiable information, there is no profile to delete. If you believe a submission you made should be removed for any reason, contact us via the footer email with approximate submission details and we will act in good faith.

7. Security

All data is stored on a private server with access restricted to the platform operator. We use industry-standard practices to prevent unauthorised access.

8. Changes

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Material changes will be noted by updating the "Last updated" date above.
